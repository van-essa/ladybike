Spinning

Available: Everyday
Start Time: 07:00 AM
Ends: 08:00 AM
Calories burnt: 400-500
Class Number: One

In spin class, you pedal along on a stationary bike as the instructor guides you through a visualization of an outdoor workout. The pace and speed will vary throughout the workout, sometimes requiring break-neck speed, and other times pedaling happens from a slow, standing position.

Ride That Hill

Available: Everyday
Start Time: 11:30 AM
Ends: 12:30 PM
Calories burnt: 500-600
Class Number: Two

Ride that hill is an indoor cycling climbing drill where you simulate a hill that creeps in grade into a very steep mountain like grade by the end. Whilst cycling up hills seated, you engage your quads, glutes and calves more than you would if you were cycling on flat terrain. A great class to increase your strength and power.

Ladybike

Available: Everyday
Start Time: 18:00 PM
Ends: 19:00 PM
Calories burnt: 600-700
Class Number: Three

Is an intense combo class that will push your body to the limit. It combines intervals of cardio with strength conditioning, moving quickly between the bike and equipment on the ground. Expect to work with dumbbells, bands, gliders, and your own body-weight.
