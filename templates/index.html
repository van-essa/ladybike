Meet Our Trainers

Vanessa Karra

Co-founder / Personal Trainer
I’m Vanessa, personal trainer and co-founder of LadyBike. I gained my qualifications in 2008 and started my career as a personal and cycling trainer for women. My goal has always been to empower women by giving them the practical tools they need to improve their health, cycling skills and fitness and feel more confident. You can find me on the LadyBike classes!

Emma Thomas

Olympic Cyclist / Personal Trainer
Hello! I’m Emma — wife, mother and an Olympic Cyclist. After having my baby, Alexandra, I decided to take a break from competitions and focus more on sharing my experience, passion for cycling, and healthy lifestyle with other women. I love what I do and what better to share it with you? You can find me on the Ride that hill and Spinning classes!
